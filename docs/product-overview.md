# Bury It! Product Overview

## One-Line Pitch

`Bury It!` is a fully anonymous digital graveyard where people bury messages, notes, memories, and emotional clutter they cannot quite delete.

## Short Description

`Bury It!` helps users let go without erasing everything forever. Instead of deleting a painful note, an old draft, a regret, or a chat fragment, they can bury it. Each burial becomes a small memorial with a tombstone style, a category, and a short epitaph.

Users can keep a burial private or anonymously place it in the public graveyard, where others can quietly explore, pay respect, and feel less alone.

## Product Principles

1. Anonymity is the product, not a feature.
2. Emotional safety matters more than virality.
3. Text is enough. V1 does not need images.
4. Ritual creates value. The burial flow should feel intentional.
5. Public discovery should feel haunting and human, not chaotic.

## Core Value Proposition

For people who keep digital things they no longer want to see, `Bury It!` offers a gentle ritual for letting go through anonymous burial, emotional categorization, and optional public memorialization.

## Positioning

`Bury It!` sits between:

- emotional journaling
- anonymous social products
- digital decluttering
- memorial-style ritual experiences

It is not a storage tool, not a therapy app, and not a gossip platform. It is a ritualized space for digital release.

## Audience

### Primary Audience

- People aged 18-35 who hold onto old messages, drafts, notes, and personal digital clutter
- Users who enjoy anonymous products with emotional honesty
- People going through endings, transitions, or identity shifts

### Secondary Audience

- Users who browse anonymous confession-style content
- People interested in emotional self-reflection
- Users who enjoy atmospheric social experiences

## The Emotional Job To Be Done

"Help me put this away in a way that feels meaningful."

## Brand Voice

- Quiet
- Poetic
- Controlled
- Respectful
- Slightly dark, never edgy for the sake of it

## Suggested Taglines

- `Bury what you can't delete.`
- `A place for digital goodbyes.`
- `Let it rest.`

## V1 Product Summary

Users can:

- create an anonymous account
- write or paste text they want to bury
- choose a category
- choose a tombstone style
- write an epitaph
- decide whether the burial is private or public
- browse anonymous public burials
- react with respectful gestures

## What Makes It Different

- Full anonymity from day one
- No image dependence
- A ritualized, emotionally rich action instead of plain posting
- Public content framed as memorials rather than social updates
