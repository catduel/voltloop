VoltLoop Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

VoltLoop is designed as a simple offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal information.

Data Collection

VoltLoop does not collect personal data, account information, location data, contacts, photos, or analytics data.

Local Game Data

The game may save progress, unlocked levels, best times, move counts, scores, sound settings, and premium unlock status locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

In-App Purchase

VoltLoop offers a one-time non-consumable In-App Purchase called Full Unlock. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children

VoltLoop is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us through the support link listed on the App Store product page.
